## Footers

Footers are used to provide additional information about the website or application, such as
copyright information, links to social media profiles, and contact information. They are typically
located at the bottom of the page and can be designed to be visually distinct from the main content.
SEO best practices suggest that footers should include important links and information that users
may need to access frequently, such as contact information, privacy policies, and terms of service.
Additionally, footers can be used to improve the overall user experience by providing easy access to
important information and resources.
